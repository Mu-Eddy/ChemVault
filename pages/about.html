About ChemVault

ChemVault is an independent academic chemistry portal for local-first chemical records, research workflows and verified scientific source enrichment.

Project focus
  1. Searchable chemistry records
  2. Research workflow tools
  3. Academic source enrichment
what it is What is ChemVault?

ChemVault is a chemistry knowledge portal that combines curated local chemical records, searchable compound data, reagent notes, material profiles, mechanisms and academic source links in one public interface.

purpose Why was it created?

It was created to make chemistry research information easier to search, compare and verify. The project is designed around local-first records, clear provenance and fast access to trusted scientific databases.

features Main functions

ChemVault supports compound search, reagent dossiers, materials records, mechanism browsing, spectroscopy notes, research pages, D1-backed APIs, fallback local data and academic enrichment from sources such as PubChem and PubMed.

roadmap Future planning

Future development will expand the verified database, improve structure and hazard coverage, add more scientific source checks, strengthen search quality and build more workflow tools for chemistry learning and research.
